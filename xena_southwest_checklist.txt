METHODOLOGY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Included if the species description places it (even partially) in the
Desert Southwest: AZ, NM, UT, NV, CA, CO, or TX.
Excluded only if the description places it exclusively in FL, PR, the
Caribbean, or the Midwest/Eastern US with no Southwest range at all.
